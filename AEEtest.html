Mathematical Problems: Elasticity

1. Price Elasticity of Demand (Malawi Context)

When the price of maize in Lilongwe increased from MWK 150 per kg to MWK 180 per kg, the quantity demanded decreased from 5000 kg to 4000 kg per month. Calculate the price elasticity of demand.

Formula: PED = (% Change in Quantity Demanded) / (% Change in Price)

2. Income Elasticity of Demand (Malawi Context)

In Blantyre, when average household income increased from MWK 50,000 to MWK 60,000 per month, demand for rice increased from 2000 kg to 2500 kg per month. Calculate the income elasticity of demand.

Formula: YED = (% Change in Quantity Demanded) / (% Change in Income)

3. Cross-Price Elasticity of Demand (Malawi Context)

When the price of Airtel mobile data bundles decreased by 10%, the demand for TNM data bundles decreased by 5%. Calculate the cross-price elasticity of demand.

Formula: XED = (% Change in Quantity Demanded of Good A) / (% Change in Price of Good B)
